4理解 Comprehension

讀完短文（生字 hover 看中文），挑戰 4 題：前 2 題細節（句中直接找）、後 2 題推理（綜合全文）。

Last Sunday I went to the zoo with my family. We saw many animals. Tigers are my favorite最喜愛的 animals. My younger sister妹妹 likes bears and monkeys. It was a sunny day, and we took拍 (拍照) many pictures. We had a good time玩得開心.
